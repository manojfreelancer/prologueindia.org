General Faq's

Is Prologue a legal entity?

Yes, we work within the preview of the Indian Laws

Does Prologue receive any perks from the Government of India?

No, we are an NGO dependent mainly on our own resources and funds raised from our believers to cater our program costs and goals. However, we have few exemptions from the Government which help us in fetching our operational costs.

How does Prologue raise funds?

Donations, selling products through various channels, providing services to individuals and corporate houses. Social entrepreneurship - Working towards innovative approaches.

Are all the resources entirely through business?

We also seek donations from individuals and corporates.

Does Prologue pay their employees?

No, all the employees working with Prologue contribute as part of their social responsibility. However, for roles requiring technical skill we often hire people to get the work done for maintaining efficient delivery system at the grass root level.

What are the pre-requisites to bag an opportunity to volunteer with us?

None, all you need is the will to work for the needy.

Do you provide any certificate for volunteering?

Yes, we believe in appreciation, motivation and recognition for a better society

Does Volunteering help me?

You will be surprised to see our partner companies calling you back for opportunities as well and grit with motivation is a rare quality to find. Being an alumni of Prologue exposes you to 40 plus top organizations in Hyderabad.

Will my small donations make a difference?

Rain starts with a drop so does your donation. Small contributions sum up to achieve our bigger objectives.

How genuine is Prologue?

We are not a group of people, we are a blend of ideas working towards making the world a better place to live. Prologue yearns to be a thought driven motion rather than fund fueled campaign promising to deliver goodies and steering your sorrows off. We renew, replenish and refresh with every new volunteer who joins us. We love believing in the fact that change is for good and try to make it as best as possible with our little contributions while we embrace traditional social organizations through sustainable approach.
